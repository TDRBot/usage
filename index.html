Games

You are allowed to:

  • Make fangames/mod them, long as credit is given
  • Have MINIMAL monetization (e.g. Making a fangame and accepting donations)
  • Use the characters/universe for SFW purposes!
  • If you take commissions and someone commissions art of my characters, it's chill! We gotta make a buck somehow. (Though, make sure it's SFW!)
  • Hell, pirate my paid games. It's chill.
  • Rip assets for redistribution elsewhere (i.e. The Spriters Resource)

You are NOT allowed to:

  • Charge up-front for fangames/mods
  • Claim the game as yours or redistribute it. (The Internet Archive is exempt from this!)
  • Use the characters/universe for NSFW purposes. (Seriously, don't... most of my characters are under 18.)

Music (TDRPM)

Quick note: If you use my music and receive a claim, send a dispute to RouteNote and include a link to this page. Everyone is free to use my music however you want!

You are allowed to:

  • Use in non-comercial projects. (Usage in YouTube videos with monetization is allowed, just give credit)
  • Remixes, covers, etc, are allowed.
  • Using melodies and stuff is chill, even for commercial use. (e.g. Publishing such on Spotify.)
  • ALSO you can pirate my music, though if you want to support, just buy my music on Bandcamp!

You are NOT allowed to:

  • Redistribute my music to other platforms without permission.
  • Claim you made my music.
  • Use my music in a commercial project not explicitly allowed on this page.
